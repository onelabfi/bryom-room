# Bryom Room — Playtest Guide

Thanks for helping test this. The goal is **not** to find bugs — it's to find things that **feel wrong** for your nervous system. Code fixes are cheap. Getting the feel right is the product.

## Setup

1. Open **[the playtest URL]** on your **phone**.
2. For zones and games, turn your phone **sideways (landscape)**. The entry screen stays vertical.
3. Nothing to install. No login. No account.

## What we're testing

### Flow
- Does "How are you feeling?" feel like a fair question, or does it miss how you actually feel?
- Does the recommended zone match what you needed?
- Is the entry → zone → game → "how do you feel now?" loop easy to follow?

### Each zone

**Focus** — should feel like *warming up attention without pressure.*
- Do you feel pulled along, or pushed?
- Any failure state accidentally sneaking in ("oh no, I messed up")?

**Calm** — should feel like *lowering the volume of your nervous system.*
- Does the breathing pace match a breath that feels natural to you?
- Is anything accidentally activating (bright, fast, loud)?

**Fidget** — should feel like *somewhere for the hands to go.*
- Satisfying? Hollow? Boring? (All are useful signals.)
- Do you forget time, or check the exit after 30 seconds?

**Release** — should feel like *pressure moves out.*
- Is there anything that leaves you MORE dysregulated than when you started?
- Does the 30s breathing outro help you land softly, or does it feel abrupt?

### Sensory
- Anything too bright, too fast, too loud, too flashy?
- Anything you wish you could turn down? (Settings gear in top-right of games)
- Any haptic that felt jarring vs. grounding?

### Mechanical
- Taps that didn't register
- Anything janky or laggy
- Orientation problems (rotate-prompt wrong, game doesn't fit screen)
- Text too small / too big

## How to send feedback

Every non-game screen has a small **"FEEDBACK"** pill in the bottom-right corner. Tap it anytime, type what's on your mind, send. It auto-includes:
- Which screen you were on
- Your device and viewport size
- An anonymous session id so we can stitch a full session

After any game, the "How do you feel now?" screen also has a **"Report something"** link.

**Send whatever comes to mind**, even if it feels like a small thing. "The orb is the wrong shade of green" is a real signal.

## Things we *know* are rough

- All graphics are placeholders. Reindeer, sprites, colors — all waiting for the Bryom art pack.
- Some games are rough first drafts. Feel is more important than polish.
- Calm and Fidget have no duration cap on purpose. Leave when you're done.
- Release games hard-cap at 90s. That's intentional (regulation, not reward).

## Honest feelings we want to hear

- "This made me feel worse."
- "I got bored and stopped."
- "I don't understand why this is here."
- "I'd never share this with anyone."
- "I played Smash Field for 3 rounds in a row, is that bad?"

Those are the messages that make the product better. Polite feedback is a polite product.

## Safety

If anything at any point feels dysregulating — close the tab. That's the right call, not a failure. Tell us after if you can.

Thanks for doing this.
